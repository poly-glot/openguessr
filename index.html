How it works

Simple, fast, competitive

Drop into a Street View, figure out which country you're in, and score points for speed and accuracy. Compete with friends on a live leaderboard.

  1. Create a game

    One click to start. You get a unique game link instantly.

  2. Share the link

    Send the URL to friends. They join without signing up.

  3. Guess the country

    See a Street View panorama and pick the correct country flag.

  4. See scores

    Points for correct guesses plus speed bonus. 5 rounds per game.
